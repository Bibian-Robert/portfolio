  <h2>Accessibility under Extreme Weather</h2>
  <p>This project quantified flood-induced changes in geographical accessibility to health services in Kenya. Methods included travel-time modeling, population coverage loss calculations, and scenario analysis to inform climate-resilient health planning.</p>
